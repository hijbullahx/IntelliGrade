The IntelliGrade Process

From script upload to final grade, our transparent AI pipeline is designed for accuracy and instructor oversight.

1

Upload & OCR

Teacher uploads scanned answer scripts. Our system performs OCR to digitize the text.

2

Answer Parsing & Rubric Matching

The system intelligently segments answers and matches them to the corresponding question rubric.

3

AI Evaluation via Gemini

A structured prompt is sent to the LLM, which returns a suggested score, explanation, and feedback in a consistent format.

4

Confidence Score & Validation

Each evaluation is assigned a confidence score. Low-confidence results are flagged for mandatory review.

Final Teacher Review & Approval

The teacher reviews all suggestions, makes any necessary adjustments, and gives the final approval before results are published.
